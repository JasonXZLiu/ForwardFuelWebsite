Our SOLUTION

Our biodiesel has a seal of excellence, due to our extensive previous research into the chemistry of the process and our connections to chemical engineers. To prove our standard, all of Forward Fuel’s biodiesel is tested and analyzed by our in-staff chemical engineer to prove that it is up to par and worthy to be in your car. Forward Fuel not only produces high quality biodiesel, we also offer a complete service which caters both to fast-food restaurants and gas companies who are looking for an extra avenue of revenue.

For Potential Partner Restaurants:

The part of our complete process in which you would be involved would be the very start. Every week a Forward Fuel employee would visit your restaurant to collect all your excess and/or waste oil. Your monetary incentive for this partnership would consist of a payment of 10 cents per liter of oil we receive.

For Potential Partner Gas Stations:

Once all the processes to turn the oil into biodiesel are complete, the biodiesel is ready to be sold to partner gas stations. With your partnership we will sell you our transformed biodiesel in a 100% concentration for you to mix with diesel as you see fit. Per litre this biodiesel will cost you 55 cents, allowing you as our partners to still make a profit.

Why buy biodiesel?

Biodiesel positively affects the environment as well as the `economy. Not only is biodiesel a cheap option for individual customers and large gas stations alike, it also saves money for restaurants. Instead of having to pay to have companies dispose of waste oil, restaurants are now making a little bit of money selling us the waste oil necessary to make our fuel. Because paying to dispose of oil that could in fact be making money just doesn’t make sense. Of course there are the environmental benefits to, and not just in the manner that biodiesel has decreased carbon dioxide emissions for roughly the same fuel economy. Biodiesel improves the air quality around us but is also simply a more sustainable method of powering engines.
