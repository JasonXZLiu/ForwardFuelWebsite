"HOW MIGHT CANADIANS REDUCE THEIR INDIVIDUAL ENERGY FOOTPRINT?"

The ISSUE

Global warming is an international issue, and one that Canada contributes to greatly. In 2016, Canadian greenhouse gas emissions accounted for 725 megatonnes of carbon dioxide being released into the atmosphere and 26% of that figure was due to vehicles. The solution for this issue is relatively simple, every kilogram of our biodiesel used saves 0.31 kg of carbon dioxide from being released into the atmosphere. Forward Fuel’s mission is to provide customers with a greener form of fuel, a biodiesel produced by used and waste oil.
